---
name: Yoga
summary: Moving practice of posture, breath and focus — mobility and stress regulation in one.
instructions:
  - Start with a style that fits your goal (Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin).
  - Let the breath lead the movement — inhale to open, exhale to deepen.
  - Align each pose cleanly; shorter and clean beats long and cramped.
  - Finish with shavasana (lying still) — it's part of the practice.
tips:
  - Beginners benefit more from a guided class (studio or video) than from improvising.
  - 20-30 minutes, 3× a week, already produces noticeable mobility gains.
