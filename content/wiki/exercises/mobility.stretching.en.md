---
name: Stretching
summary: Static stretching after training — improves range of motion long-term.
instructions:
  - Only when muscles are warm (after training or light warm-up).
  - Move into each stretch slowly until you feel a light pull (never pain).
  - Hold each position 30-60 seconds quietly, breathing evenly.
  - 2-3 repeats per muscle per session — no cold-stretching from standing.
tips:
  - Avoid static stretching right before heavy strength or speed work (leave 10-20 min).
  - Flexibility takes weeks — patience beats intensity.
---
