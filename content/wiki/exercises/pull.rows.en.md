---
name: Rows
summary: Horizontal pulling — counterpart to pressing and a key to healthy shoulder posture.
instructions:
  - 'Bodyweight: hang under a low bar or TRX, body in a straight line.'
  - 'Weighted: hinge over with a flat back, weight near shin height.'
  - Initiate with the shoulder blades — retract first, then pull the elbows toward the hips.
  - Lower back under control until the shoulder blades feel long again.
tips:
  - Pull to the lower ribs to bias the lats; pull to the chest for the mid-back.
  - No kipping — the trunk stays still, only the arms and upper back move.
