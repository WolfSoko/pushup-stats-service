---
name: Dead Bug
summary: Supine anti-extension drill — coordinates a braced core with contralateral arm and leg motion.
instructions:
  - Lie on your back, arms pointing straight up, knees bent 90° over the hips.
  - Press the lower back firmly into the floor — no gap between back and mat.
  - Slowly lower the opposite arm and leg at the same time without lifting the back.
  - Return under control, then switch sides.
tips:
  - If the lower back lifts off, shorten the range or keep the knee bent higher.
  - Tempo matters more than range — 3-4 s per side beats a rushed back-and-forth.
