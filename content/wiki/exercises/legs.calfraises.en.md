---
name: Calf Raises
summary: Isolated calf training — jump power and stability for ankles and knees.
instructions:
  - Stand tall, feet hip-width, weight on the balls of the feet.
  - Press slowly up onto the toes, pause briefly at the top.
  - Lower under control — do not let the heels crash into the floor.
  - Standing on a step edge unlocks the full range of motion.
tips:
  - Stand on a step (heels hanging) for much more stretch and stimulus.
  - High reps work better for calves — aim for 15-25 per set.
---
