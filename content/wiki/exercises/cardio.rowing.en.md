---
name: Rowing (Indoor)
summary: Full-body cardio on the ergometer — legs, back and conditioning in one stroke.
instructions:
  - 'Catch: knees bent, shins vertical, arms extended, torso slightly forward.'
  - 'Drive: push the legs first, then swing the torso back, finally pull the arms to the lower ribs.'
  - "Finish: legs straight, torso leaning back slightly (~11 o'clock), hands at the lower ribs."
  - 'Recovery: reverse the sequence — arms extend, torso forward, knees bend.'
tips:
  - 60-70 % of the power comes from the legs — don't over-pull with the arms.
  - Target stroke rate 18-26 spm for endurance; higher for intervals.
---
