---
name: Jump Rope
summary: High-intensity cardio with minimal gear — calf strength, coordination and conditioning.
instructions:
  - Size the rope so the handles reach mid-sternum when you stand on the middle of it.
  - Stand tall, elbows close to the body, the motion comes from the wrists.
  - Small, fast hops — just enough to clear the rope (1-2 cm).
  - Land on the balls of the feet, knees absorbing — no heel stomping.
tips:
  - Beginners count reps in sets (3 × 50); advanced over time (3 × 60 s).
  - Don't force double-unders — get a clean basic rhythm first, then add speed.
---
