---
name: Hip Opener
summary: Mobilisation routine for the hips — essential for desk-bound jobs and deep squats.
instructions:
  - From all-fours bring one leg forward into the 90/90 position.
  - Extend the back leg straight, both hip points square to the floor.
  - Lean forward over the front leg for more glute stretch; gentle back-and-forth.
  - Rotate through pigeon, frog and butterfly for full hip mobility.
tips:
  - 5 minutes daily beats 30 minutes once a week.
  - Breathe into the stretch — release a little more on every exhale.
