---
name: Leg Raises
summary: Lying or hanging leg raises — emphasise the lower portion of the abdominal wall.
instructions:
  - Either lie on your back or hang from a pull-up bar.
  - Legs straight or slightly bent, lift them slowly toward the ceiling.
  - Lumbar spine (lying) or torso (hanging) stays still — no swinging.
  - Lower under control, no slamming into the floor and no kipping at the bottom.
tips:
  - 'Progression: knees tucked → knees to chest → straight legs to horizontal.'
  - In the hang, actively depress the shoulder blades or the shoulder girdle ends up doing the work.
