---
name: Dips
summary: Vertical pressing on parallel bars or rings — chest, triceps and shoulders in one move.
instructions:
  - Support on two parallel bars, arms extended, legs free.
  - Actively depress the shoulder blades — do not hang in the joints.
  - Lower the body under control, elbows tracking slightly back, until upper arm is parallel.
  - Press back up powerfully, do not lock the elbows.
tips:
  - Lean slightly forward to bias the chest; stay upright to bias the triceps.
  - 'Beginners: start with bench dips, then negative dips (eccentric only), then full dips.'
---
