---
name: Crunches
summary: Short upper-back curl — isolates the rectus abdominis without the hip flexors taking over.
instructions:
  - Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat on the floor.
  - Hands lightly behind the ears or crossed over the chest.
  - Lift only the shoulder blades off the floor — the lower back stays grounded.
  - Lower under control, never fully losing tension at the bottom.
tips:
  - Chin loose toward the chest, but do not actively pull — the abs do the work, not the neck.
  - Add a 1-second hold at the top to increase the stimulus at the same rep count.
---
