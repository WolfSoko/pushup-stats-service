---
name: High Knees
summary: Running in place with high knee drive — heart rate booster and hip flexor primer.
instructions:
  - Stand tall, arms at boxer angle.
  - Alternate driving the knees up to hip height (or higher) at a fast pace.
  - Land on the balls of the feet, springy and quiet.
  - Stay upright — don't lean forward to "meet" the knees.
tips:
  - Great cardio burst between sets (30-60 s) — no gear, plenty of stimulus.
  - If the knees don't come up, it's jogging in place — not the exercise.
---
