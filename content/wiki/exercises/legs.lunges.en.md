---
name: Lunges
summary: Single-leg strength in a split stance — power, balance and side-to-side symmetry.
instructions:
  - Stand tall, take a long step forward.
  - Lower the back knee under control toward the floor, front knee stacked over the ankle.
  - Both knees finish at roughly 90° — the front shin stays vertical.
  - Drive through the front heel back to standing; switch sides or step.
tips:
  - Stay upright — torso must not collapse forward.
  - 'Variants: reverse lunges are easier on the knees, walking lunges add a balance demand.'
---
