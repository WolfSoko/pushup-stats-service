---
name: Cycling
summary: Joint-friendly endurance training on road, trail or indoor bike.
instructions:
  - Set the saddle so the knee stays slightly (10-15°) bent at the bottom — no fully extended leg.
  - Cadence 80-100 rpm for efficient pedalling — less torque, less knee wear.
  - Hands relaxed on the bar, shoulders down — not shrugged.
  - Adapt breathing to the pace.
tips:
  - Gauge effort with power (watts) or heart rate — pure "feel" is misleading.
  - Clip pedals raise efficiency but need a few rides to get used to.
---
