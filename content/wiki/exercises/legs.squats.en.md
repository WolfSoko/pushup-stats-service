---
name: Squats
summary: King of leg exercises — quads, glutes and core in a single pattern.
instructions:
  - Shoulder-width stance, toes turned slightly outward.
  - Send the hips back, then bend the knees over the toes — like sitting on an invisible chair.
  - Descend as deep as mobility and hips allow — ideally thighs parallel or below.
  - Drive back up powerfully through the heels; do not lock the knees at the top.
tips:
  - Track the knees over the toes — do not let them cave inward.
  - Squeeze the glutes on the way up or the lower back ends up overworking.
---
