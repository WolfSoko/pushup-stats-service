---
name: Mountain Climbers
summary: Fast alternating knee drives from a push-up position — abs, hip flexors and cardio in one.
instructions:
  - High plank like a push-up start, hands under the shoulders, body in a straight line.
  - Pull one knee to the chest, then return it as the other drives forward.
  - Tempo is your choice — fast for cardio, slow for more abdominal tension.
  - Hips stay low — no piking up or rocking side to side.
tips:
  - Count "left + right = 1 rep" so logs stay consistent.
  - 'Variant: cross-body (knee to the opposite elbow) hits the obliques more.'
---
