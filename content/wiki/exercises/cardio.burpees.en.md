---
name: Burpees
summary: Full-body cardio from squat to push-up to jump — the toughest no-equipment standard.
instructions:
  - Stand tall, drop into a squat and place the hands in front of the feet.
  - Jump both legs back into a plank, optionally perform one push-up.
  - Jump the legs forward under the body, back into a squat.
  - Explode up into a jump, hands clapping briefly over the head.
tips:
  - Choose tempo by training age — form beats speed.
  - 'Scaling: skip the push-up; replace the jump with standing up — both make it accessible.'
---
