---
name: Dead Hang
summary: Passive hang from a bar — grip strength, shoulder mobility and spinal decompression.
instructions:
  - Grip a pull-up bar shoulder-width, thumbs outside the bar (or wrapped) — both feet off the floor.
  - Keep the shoulders actively down and back — no shrugged, sunken position.
  - Trunk relaxed but no lower-back arch — legs still, no swinging.
  - Hold as long as form stays clean, then lower under control.
tips:
  - 'Beginners: 3 × 15-20 s; advanced 3 × 45-60 s — long-term target 1-2 minutes straight.'
  - Sensitive shoulders? Use an active hang (scapulae depressed) instead of passive.
---
