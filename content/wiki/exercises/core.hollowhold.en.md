---
name: Hollow Hold
summary: Static "hollow body" position with arms and legs raised — a foundation for gymnastic strength.
instructions:
  - Lie on your back, arms and legs extended straight.
  - Lift shoulders and legs simultaneously so only the lower back touches the floor.
  - Actively press the lumbar spine into the floor — the dish ("hollow") must stay.
  - Hold the position, breathing evenly throughout.
tips:
  - 'Scale from easy to hard: tuck (knees pulled in) → half-hollow (knees 90°) → full hollow (extended).'
  - 'Beginners: 3 × 15-20 s; advanced 3 × 45-60 s.'
---
