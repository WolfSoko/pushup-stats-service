---
name: Foam Rolling
summary: Self-massage on a foam roller — releases tension and improves circulation.
instructions:
  - Place the target muscle on the roller, control your body weight over it.
  - Roll slowly (2-3 cm per second), pause 20-30 s on tender spots.
  - Breathe evenly — never hold the breath through discomfort; aim for 5-6/10 intensity, not more.
  - 30-60 s per major muscle group, longer when needed.
tips:
  - Short (1-2 min per area) before training, longer and more relaxing afterwards.
  - Don't roll directly on bones or joints — muscle bellies only.
