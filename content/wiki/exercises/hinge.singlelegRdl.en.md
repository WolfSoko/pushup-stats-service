---
name: Single-Leg Romanian Deadlift
summary: One-legged hip hinge — balance, posterior chain and hip stability in one drill.
instructions:
  - Stand tall, weight on one leg, the other lightly lifted.
  - Send the hips back and tilt the torso forward — the rear leg extends back as a counterweight.
  - Keep a flat back, standing knee soft; hinge to horizontal or as far as form allows.
  - Drive through the heel back to standing while pushing the hips forward.
tips:
  - Holding a weight (dumbbell, water bottle) in the opposite hand raises the stability demand.
  - Allow a fingertip on a wall in the beginning — single-leg balance takes practice.
