---
name: Plank
summary: Isometric hold on forearms or hands — the gold standard for core stability.
instructions:
  - Forearms or palms shoulder-width on the floor, elbows directly under the shoulders.
  - Body forms a straight line from head to heels; actively brace glutes and abs.
  - Pull the shoulders away from the ears, neck long, gaze between the hands.
  - Hold the position — no sagging, no lower-back arch, no piked hips.
tips:
  - Keep breathing evenly — do not hold your breath.
  - 'Beginners: 3 × 20-30 s, advanced 3 × 60-90 s; stop the moment form breaks.'
---
