---
name: Jump Squats
summary: Explosive squat with a vertical jump — speed-strength and lower-body power.
instructions:
  - Lower into a regular squat.
  - Drive up explosively from the bottom, arms swinging dynamically.
  - Land soft on the mid-foot, knees absorbing — straight into the next squat.
  - Control over speed — if form breaks, rest or drop to regular squats.
tips:
  - Don't start from a tall stance — the pre-stretch at the bottom of the squat is what creates jump height.
  - Hard landings are tough on joints; a mat or cushioned shoes help.
---
