---
name: Wall Sit
summary: Isometric squat with the back on a wall — pure quad burn.
instructions:
  - Stand with the back flat against a wall, feet about hip-width, one stride forward.
  - Slide down the wall until the knees are bent to 90°.
  - Thighs parallel to the floor, knees stacked over the ankles.
  - Hold the position — pressure even on both heels, abs lightly engaged.
tips:
  - 'Beginners: 3 × 20-30 s; advanced 3 × 60-90 s.'
  - If knees complain, keep the hips slightly higher — 90° is not mandatory.
---
