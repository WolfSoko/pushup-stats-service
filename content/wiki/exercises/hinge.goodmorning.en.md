---
name: Good Morning
summary: Hip hinge with an upright torso — stretches and strengthens the posterior chain.
instructions:
  - Shoulder-width stance, hands behind the head, on the shoulders (or a bar across the upper back).
  - Knees minimally bent, push the hips back slowly.
  - Tilt the torso forward under control with a flat back — until the hamstrings tell you they're working.
  - Drive through the heels back to standing.
tips:
  - Mobility limits depth — never force a lower-back arch or rounded back.
  - 3-1-1 tempo (3 s down, 1 s pause, 1 s up) maximises stimulus with little weight.
---
