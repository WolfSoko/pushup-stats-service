---
name: Face Pull
summary: High pull with a band or cable — trains the rear shoulders and counters pressing imbalances.
instructions:
  - Anchor a band or cable at eye height, grip the ends with both hands.
  - Stand tall with arms forward; step back one or two paces to put tension on the band.
  - Pull the elbows up and out toward the ears, thumbs end pointing back.
  - Pause briefly at the end (1 s), then return under control.
tips:
  - Shoulders stay down — no shrugging the shoulder blades up.
  - Ideal companion to any pressing session; 15-25 reps per set.
---
