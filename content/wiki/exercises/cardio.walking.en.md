---
name: Walking
summary: Brisk walking outdoors or on a treadmill — the most underrated cardio stimulus.
instructions:
  - Walk tall, steps natural, arms swinging.
  - Brisk pace — you can still talk but not sing.
  - Breathe through the nose if you can; switch to mouth+nose on inclines.
  - 30-60 minutes per session for endurance, or several short walks across the day.
tips:
  - 6-10 km/h on flat ground; hills bump the stimulus significantly.
  - Footwear matters more than pace — bad shoes wreck your gait after 30 minutes.
---
