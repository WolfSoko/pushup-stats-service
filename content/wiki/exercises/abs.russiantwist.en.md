---
name: Russian Twist
summary: Seated torso rotation — rotational strength and obliques.
instructions:
  - Sit on your glutes, knees bent, feet either lifted or grounded.
  - Lean the torso slightly back with a flat (not rounded) back.
  - Hands clasped in front of the chest (or holding a weight); rotate left–right rhythmically.
  - Movement comes from the trunk, not from the arms.
tips:
  - Load with a dumbbell, kettlebell or water bottle to scale up.
  - One rep = one twist to the left AND one to the right.
---
