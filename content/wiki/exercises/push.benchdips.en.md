---
name: Bench Dips
summary: Triceps dips with hands on a bench — accessible entry variant.
instructions:
  - Stand with your back to a bench, hands shoulder-width on the bench edge.
  - Slide the hips forward off the bench, legs extended or bent in front.
  - Bend the elbows to about 90° under control, hips sinking straight down (not forward).
  - Press back up with the triceps, do not lock out the elbows.
tips:
  - Protect the shoulders — don't go deeper than feels good.
  - Make it harder with straight legs, feet on a second bench, or weight on the lap.
---
