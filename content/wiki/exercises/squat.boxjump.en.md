---
name: Box Jumps
summary: Jump onto a sturdy elevation — speed-strength, coordination and confidence.
instructions:
  - Stand in front of a stable box (or bench, low stool).
  - Drop into a quarter-squat, arms swinging back.
  - Drive up explosively from the squat, arms swinging forward.
  - Land soft on both feet on the box, knees absorbing — then step (don't jump) back down.
tips:
  - Pick a height you can clear safely — most box-jump injuries come from going too high.
  - Step down between reps — landing repeatedly from height is hard on knees and Achilles.
---
