---
name: Jumping Jacks
summary: Classic warm-up and cardio move — shoulders, hips and heart rate in one.
instructions:
  - Stand tall, feet together, arms by the sides.
  - In one jump, spread the legs sideways while raising the arms overhead.
  - On the next jump, return to the start.
  - Keep it rhythmic, landing soft on the balls of the feet.
tips:
  - Perfect warm-up before push-up or running sessions — 2-3 minutes is enough.
  - Knees complaining? Step-touch instead of jumping for a low-impact variant.
---
