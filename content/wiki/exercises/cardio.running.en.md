---
name: Running
summary: Classic endurance training — the easiest path to better conditioning and VO₂max.
instructions:
  - Relaxed posture, steps natural, torso tilted slightly forward.
  - Land on the mid-foot (not pure heel) to reduce impact.
  - Breathe evenly — on easy runs 3 steps in, 3 out; at harder paces 2/2.
  - Increase weekly mileage by no more than 10 % (the 10 % rule).
tips:
  - Beginners start with walk/run intervals (1 min run, 1 min walk).
  - Shoes and a running watch help, but consistency beats any gear.
