---
name: Glute Bridge
summary: Hip extension from supine — direct work for the glutes and posterior chain.
instructions:
  - Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat and hip-width on the floor.
  - Squeeze the glutes and drive the hips up to the ceiling.
  - Hold the top briefly (1 s) — body forms a line from knees to shoulders.
  - Lower under control — touch down briefly, then go again.
tips:
  - Drive through the heels, not the toes — toe-pressure shifts the load to the quads.
  - Progress with a single-leg variant or added weight once bodyweight is too easy.
