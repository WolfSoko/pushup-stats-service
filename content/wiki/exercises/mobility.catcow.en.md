---
name: Cat-Cow
summary: Spinal mobilisation in a quadruped position — alternates between rounded and arched back.
instructions:
  - Start on hands and knees — hands under shoulders, knees under hips.
  - 'Inhale, cow: drop the belly, lift the chest and gaze up and forward.'
  - 'Exhale, cat: round the spine to the ceiling, chin to chest, tail tucked.'
  - Move with the breath for 8-12 reps — no fast, jerky transitions.
tips:
  - Perfect before and after long sitting — mobilises the whole spine.
  - Movement should flow, not snap between end positions.
---
