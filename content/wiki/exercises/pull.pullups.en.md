---
name: Pull-ups
summary: Vertical pull from a bar — the king of upper-body pulling for back, biceps and grip.
instructions:
  - Hang from a pull-up bar, grip shoulder-width, palms facing forward (standard) or back (chin-up).
  - Actively depress the shoulder blades — no shoulder shrug at the bottom.
  - Pull the chest to the bar, elbows driving down and slightly back.
  - Lower under control into a full hang — no kipping.
tips:
  - 'Regression: negatives (jump up, lower slowly) or a band-assisted pull.'
  - Full range — chin over the bar at the top, elbows fully extended at the bottom.
---
